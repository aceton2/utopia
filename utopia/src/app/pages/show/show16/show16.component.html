the show in 2016

After 12 days of collaboration in the respective workshops, the artistic outcome is presented within a group show. The island-public is invited to see the show during a 4 hour open doors. There are different displays and live performances. Exhibition sights vary from indoor to sea-side. The different art pieces are joint projects, each created by a group of people. The interventions are all statements or hypotheses dealing with different topics of the theme "design your Utopia".
